Check the subject

Look out for the EXTERNAL warning. It informs you that the email is from an external source and should be treated with caution.

Ensure the subject matches the email content.

Verify the sender

Always check the sender's email address. Be cautious if it looks unfamiliar or suspicious.

Hover over the sender's name to see the full email address.

Avoid clicking links

Do not click on links in emails unless you are certain of their legitimacy.

Hover over links to see the URL before clicking.
